PEASANT LEVIE

Peasant Levie (1 each)

Represents commoners, often farmers, who have been swept up into the business of war. Often used as canon fodder in wars, can also be assigned the menial task of carrying equipment or assisting other characters.

Class Features

Levies
Peasants only act as a unit. They always attack with the skirmish combat rules, even if everyone else is engaged in man-to-man melee, and even if there is only a single peasant in the levie. All peasants controlled by a player must form up within at most three separate units. All peasants in one unit have the same core stats. If there is ambiguity about unit core stats, use the mode.
Reluctant Soldiers
Each unit of peasants in the player's control must pass a morale check at the beginning of each round of combat. If failed, that unit does not act that round. If attacked, the unit will fall back or flee.
Advancement
Those peasants who survive on the battlefield beyond 3rd level may be appointed as 1st level infantrymen, without changing their point-buy.

Starting Equipment

Gambeson, polearm (either billhook or guisarm), a hunting knife (as dagger).
