Monk (1)

A holyman who has retired from the secular world to seek spiritual contemplation. A monk is adept at gardening, cooking, and other simple crafts. Monks are a non-martial class, and have renounced worldly pursuits of violence.

It is unknown why you have left your abbey to venture into distant lands and dark tunnels.

Class Features

Blessings
A monk is not ordained and cannot say blessings, unlike the priest.
Set apart
A monk may not wield any weapon. If he chooses to use a weapon in combat, he attacks as a peasant. Afterward he must spend an hour in repentance.
Oratio vulgaris
Monks can read and speak fluent Latin. This can facilitate easier communication with foreigners.
