Class Features

Doughty
A knight need not check morale in combat.
Impetuous
A knight taunted by similarly powerful enemy combatant must fail a morale check, or else the knight will charge and engage in single combat, regardless of player's wishes.
Fantasy Combat
At 4th level, a knight may attack as a Hero on the Fantasy Combat table.

Starting Equipment

Poll axe, arming sword, shield, chainmail, horse.
