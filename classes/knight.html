Class Features

Doughty
A knight need not check morale in combat.
Impetuous
A knight taunted by similarly powerful enemy combatant must fail a morale check, or else the knight will charge and engage in single combat, regardless of player's wishes.

Attached Classes

Squire
If the same player controls a knight and one peasant, that peasant automatically becomes the knight's squire. A squire may use any class or armor or weapon, and need not check morale while the knight is in sight. A squires is expected to stay prepared in case his sire requires backup arms, and so cannot engage in combat. A squire does not gain in level while serving as a squire. If the knight is slain in combat, the squire is immediately promoted to a 1st level soldier.
