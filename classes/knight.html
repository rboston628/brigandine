Class Features

Doughty
A knight need not check morale in combat.
Impetuous
A knight taunted by similarly powerful enemy combatant must fail a morale check, or else charge and engage in man-to-man combat, regardless of player's wishes.
Squire
A single peasant may accompany a knight, and automatically becomes the knight's squire. Squires do not do anything beyond carry their knight's equipment and help into and out of armor, though may occasionally engage in combat when threatened.

Starting Equipment

Poll axe, sword, shield, chainmail, horse.
