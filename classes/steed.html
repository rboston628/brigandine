LOYAL STEED

Loyal steed (½)

This is not simply a horse. This is a highly intelligent horse which has bonded to its master. The steed may be controlled by the player even when not ridden, as opposed to a normal horse, which can only be used as a mount.

Class Features

Noble animal
A player can have at most 1 loyal steed. The steed is a noble animal for conveying chivalry; it is not a mere pack animal for hauling loot or pulling wagons.
